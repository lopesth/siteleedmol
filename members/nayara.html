    MSc. Nayara Dantas Coutinho



    Graduated in Industrial Chemistry from the Universidade Estadual de Goiás, MsC. in Physics and Chemistry from the Universidade Estadual de Goiás and Ph.D. Candidate in Chemistry from the Universidade de Brasília. I am currently working on the following topics: Simulations of quantum molecular dynamics (Car-Parrinello molecular dynamics and Born-Oppenheimer molecular dynamics) in atmospheric and technological systems, proposing new methodologies for obtaining kinetic parameters and describing unconventional mechanisms in chemical reactions.
